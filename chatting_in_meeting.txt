Chatting in the meeting
Chat with others#

The Viewer and Control Panel includes a Chat pane where you can exchange messages with other participants. You'll be notified if you receive a chat and your Chat pane is minimized.

    Everyone - Send a message to all meeting attendees
    Organizer(s) only - Send a message only to organizers and co-organizers.
    Specific attendee - Send a private message to just that person.
