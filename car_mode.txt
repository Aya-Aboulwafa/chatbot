Car mode
Mobile application#
camera

If you are having a meeting and you are in the car driving, you can easily attend it and use it without worries.

Accessibility to the functions of the meeting is eassier while driving.

Mute/unmute the voice from the mic icon, the video is stopped. When you click on “Select sound device” you can choose the desired sound for the audio.

-Leave meeting button if you want to leave the meeting.

-Select “close” to close the car mode feature.
